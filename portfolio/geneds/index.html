How do General Education Electives help?

The most important thing general electives have helped to teach me is time management. General Electives seem to have more frequent, shorter homework assignments than most of my technical electives.

Another thing is how general education electives are more for life-outside-of-work activities. For example, HD FS 276, "Human Sexuality" was a class I took in my sophomore class at Iowa State. The topics covered in that class really gave me a better perspective on the kinds of people in this world, some of which I had no idea even existed. Music 102, or "Intro to Music Listening", was a class I took my freshman year, and it exposed me to a variety of musical genres which I never would have known about otherwise, and a lot of them I really enjoy listening to
